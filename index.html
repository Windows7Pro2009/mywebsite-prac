Welcome to NHCE

New Horizon College of Engineering is an Autonomous college affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) & University Grants Commission (UGC). It is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade & National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

Located in the heart of the IT capital of India, Bangalore, NHCE is surrounded by some of the biggest IT giants like Intel, Accenture, Aricent, Cisco, JP Morgan, and Symphony.
